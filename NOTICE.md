# Notice — Licensing & Usage

## Summary

This repository is a **curation** of publicly available machine-learning educational material. It does not contain any original research or teaching content. Each document is a reformatted copy of publicly accessible material, retained here for **research and educational purposes**, with full attribution to the original author in its frontmatter and in [SOURCES.md](SOURCES.md).

## What's covered by which rights

| Component | Rights |
|---|---|
| Repository structure, `INDEX.md`, organization, any scripts | MIT License (this repo) |
| arXiv papers (`corpus/papers/`) | Retained by their authors; included under arXiv's non-exclusive distribution license. Source ID + URL in each file. |
| Lecture transcripts (`corpus/youtube/`) | Auto-generated captions of publicly available videos, included for research/educational use. All rights remain with the speakers, courses, and institutions. |
| Web articles (`corpus/web/`) | Copyright their respective authors/publishers. Included for research/educational use with attribution and source URLs. |

## Intended use

This corpus is intended for **non-commercial research and education** — e.g. building learning tools, retrieval systems, embeddings benchmarks, and personal study. If you intend to use any included content commercially, obtain permission from the original rights holders.

## Takedown / removal policy

If you are an author, instructor, institution, or rights holder and you would like your material **modified or removed**:

1. Open an issue on this repository, **or**
2. Contact the repository owner directly.

Such requests will be honored **promptly and without dispute**. The goal of this project is to help people learn and to send credit and traffic *to* creators — never to take anything away from them.

## Please support the original creators

- Watch the lectures on their official channels and subscribe.
- Take the courses.
- Read and cite the papers.
- Read the blogs at their source.

The value here is in the curation and formatting; the knowledge is entirely theirs.
